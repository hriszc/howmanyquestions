Conversion Information

1 mile = 5280 feet - This is the standard conversion used in the United States and other countries using the imperial system.

The mile is a unit of length in the imperial system, while the foot is its subunit. This conversion is commonly used in running, hiking, and distance measurements.

To convert feet to miles, divide by 5280. To convert miles to feet, multiply by 5280.
