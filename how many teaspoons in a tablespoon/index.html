Common Uses:

  • Recipe scaling and measurements
  • Cooking and baking ingredient conversion
  • Spice and seasoning measurements
  • Medication dosage calculations

Quick Tips:

  • Use 1/2 tablespoon = 1.5 teaspoons for smaller measurements
  • Most standard measuring spoons include: 1/4 tsp, 1/2 tsp, 1 tsp, 1/2 tbsp, 1 tbsp
  • This converter works in both directions automatically
  • Perfect for adjusting recipes for different serving sizes

Metric Equivalents:

  • 1 teaspoon = 4.93 milliliters (ml)
