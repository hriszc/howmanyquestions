How Many Ounces in a Cup?

This converter helps you find out how many ounces are in a cup instantly. Whether you're cooking, baking, or measuring liquids, this tool provides accurate conversions between US fluid ounces and cups.

Quick Facts:

  • 1 US cup = 8 US fluid ounces
  • 1 US fluid ounce = 0.125 US cups
  • 1 UK cup = 8.3267 UK fluid ounces
  • 1 metric cup = 8.7988 US fluid ounces
