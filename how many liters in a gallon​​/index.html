Conversion Information

1 gallon = 3.78541 liters - This is the standard US liquid gallon conversion. Note that UK gallons are different (1 UK gallon = 4.546 liters).

The gallon is a unit of volume in the imperial and US customary systems, while the liter is the standard unit in the metric system. This conversion is commonly used in cooking, fuel measurements, and everyday volume calculations.

To convert gallons to liters, multiply by 3.78541. To convert liters to gallons, divide by 3.78541.
