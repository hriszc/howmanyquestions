How many miles is a 5K?

A classic 5K road race covers five kilometers, which is exactly 5,000 meters or about three miles. This page turns that into crisp US measurements and gives you fast presets for any training distance.

Metric to imperial

5 kilometers × 0.621371 = 3.106855 miles. Rounded for everyday runners, that is 3.1 miles.

Track laps

An outdoor 400 m track requires 12.5 laps to complete a 5K. The converter keeps laps updated for any distance.

Yards & steps

5K equals 5,468 yards and roughly 6,560 steps for an average stride (about 1,312 steps per kilometer).

Questions people ask

5K FAQ

Is a 5K exactly 3 miles?

Not quite. A 5-kilometer race equals 3.106855 miles. That is 3 miles plus an extra 0.106855 miles (about 188 yards). Many runners round to 3.1 miles for quick math.

How long does it take to walk a 5K?

At a brisk 15-minute-per-mile pace, expect roughly 47 minutes. A casual 20-minute pace takes about 62 minutes. Use the converter to check distances while building your schedule.

How many laps on an indoor track?

Indoor facilities vary. Divide 5,000 meters by your track length. For a 200 m indoor loop, 5K equals 25 laps; for a 300 m loop, it is about 16.7 laps.

What about 5K in yards or feet?

5K converts to 5,468 yards or 16,404 feet. The stat cards update these numbers whenever you change the kilometer input.
