Frequently Asked Questions

How many miles is 10,000 steps?

10,000 steps is approximately 4.7 miles for an average person with a stride length of 2.5 feet.

How accurate is this calculator?

Our calculator uses the standard average stride length of 2.5 feet. Individual results may vary based on your height and walking style.

What is the average stride length?

The average stride length for adults is approximately 2.5 feet (30 inches), but this varies by height and gender.

How can I measure my exact stride length?

Walk 10 steps, measure the total distance in feet, and divide by 10. This gives your average stride length.
