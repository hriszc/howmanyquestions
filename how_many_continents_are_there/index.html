Frequently Asked Questions

Why are there 7 continents? ‚ñº

The classification of 7 continents is based on geographical, cultural, and historical factors. The seven continents are: Asia, Africa, North America, South America, Antarctica, Europe, and Australia.

What is the largest continent? ‚ñº

Asia is the largest continent, covering about 30% of Earth's total land area and hosting approximately 60% of the world's population.

Which continent has the fewest people? ‚ñº

Antarctica has the fewest permanent residents, with only researchers and scientists living there temporarily. It has no native human population.

Are Europe and Asia separate continents? ‚ñº

While Europe and Asia are geographically connected, they are considered separate continents due to cultural, historical, and political differences, as well as the Ural Mountains serving as a natural boundary.
