Conversion Information

1/4 cup = 4 tablespoons - This is the standard conversion used in US cooking and baking measurements. One cup contains 16 tablespoons, so 1/4 cup equals 4 tablespoons.

Tablespoons and cups are volume measurements commonly used in cooking and baking recipes. This conversion is essential for following recipes accurately and adjusting ingredient quantities.

To convert tablespoons to cups, divide by 16. To convert cups to tablespoons, multiply by 16. This applies to US standard measurements.
