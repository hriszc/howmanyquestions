About Quarts and Gallons

This converter helps you convert between quarts and gallons, the most common volume measurements in US cooking and liquid measurement. Whether you're following a recipe, measuring ingredients, or working with liquid volumes, this tool provides accurate conversions with support for metric measurements too.

Quick Conversion Facts

  • 1 US gallon = 4 US quarts
  • 1 US quart = 32 US fluid ounces
  • 1 US gallon = 128 US fluid ounces
  • 1 US gallon = 8 US pints
  • 1 US gallon = 16 US cups
  • 1 US gallon ≈ 3.785 liters
