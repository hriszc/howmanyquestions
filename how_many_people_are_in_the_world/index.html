Frequently asked questions

How is the live population calculated?

The tool uses the UN mid-year baseline, then applies the published annual growth rate converted to a per-second increment so the value stays in sync with official releases.

Which regions add the most people?

India remains the largest contributor in absolute numbers, while Nigeria, Pakistan, and other Sub-Saharan African countries deliver the fastest percentage gains.

Why does the counter differ from other sites?

Different organizations start from slightly different baselines or update schedules. Variance under 0.1% is normal and typically resolves after the next UN bulletin.
