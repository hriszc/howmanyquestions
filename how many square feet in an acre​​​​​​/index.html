Conversion Information

1 acre = 43,560 square feet - This is the standard conversion used in the United States and other countries using the imperial system for land measurement.

The acre is a unit of area commonly used in the imperial and US customary systems. One acre is defined as the area of one chain by one furlong (66 by 660 feet), which equals 43,560 square feet.

To convert square feet to acres, divide by 43,560. To convert acres to square feet, multiply by 43,560. This conversion is essential for real estate, land measurement, and property calculations.
