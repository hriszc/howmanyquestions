How Many Tablespoons in a Cup?

This converter helps you find out how many tablespoons are in a cup instantly. Whether you're cooking, baking, or measuring ingredients, this tool provides accurate conversions between US tablespoons and cups.

Quick Facts:

  • 1 US cup = 16 US tablespoons
  • 1 US tablespoon = 0.0625 US cups
  • 1 UK cup = 13.3228 UK tablespoons
  • 1 metric cup = 13.5149 US tablespoons
