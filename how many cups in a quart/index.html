About Cups and Quarts

A quart is equal to 4 cups, making it one of the most common volume conversions in cooking and baking. This simple relationship makes it easy to scale recipes up or down, and is essential knowledge for any home cook or professional chef.

Quick Conversion Facts

  • 1 US quart = 4 US cups
  • 1 US quart = 2 US pints
  • 1 US quart = 32 US fluid ounces
  • 1 US quart ≈ 0.946 liters
  • 1 US quart = 946.35 milliliters
