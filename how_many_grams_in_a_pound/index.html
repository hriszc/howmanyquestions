Conversion Information

1 pound = 453.592 grams - This is the standard conversion for avoirdupois pounds, commonly used in the United States and other countries.

The pound is a unit of weight in the imperial system, while the gram is the standard unit in the metric system. This conversion is essential for cooking, baking, scientific measurements, and everyday weight calculations.

To convert pounds to grams, multiply by 453.592. To convert grams to pounds, divide by 453.592.
